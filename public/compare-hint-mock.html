Hover-hint: "Compare →" on leaderboard rows

Problem: the → arrow appears on hover but gives no context — users don't know if clicking navigates, compares, or does something else.

Proposal: replace the bare → with a small "Compare →" label that slides in on hover. Same accent color, slightly smaller font, slides in from the right. Clicking any non-YOU row opens the compare view.

Design notes
Label: "Compare →" — verb + direction. Immediately communicates the action.
Appearance: slides in 6 px from the right, fades to full opacity over 150 ms. Disappears instantly on mouse-out.
YOU rows: no compare hint — clicking your own form does nothing, so the hint is omitted.
Mobile: hint is always visible (no hover state on touch). Could show as a small "⇄" icon instead of text, or stay hidden and rely on the tap action itself.
