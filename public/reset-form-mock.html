"Reset form" — clear a form to a blank slate

User-facing, in My predictions. Resetting clears every match score + the champion pick on the selected form (the form and its name stay; it becomes a fresh blank to refill). Since the form is no longer complete, it drops back to draft and will need re-submitting. Two placement options below — pick one.

Option A · Recommended
Inside a per-form "⋯" menu on the active form

A small kebab (⋯) on the selected form pill opens form-level actions — Rename, Reset, Delete — grouped together. Keeps the prediction action bar uncluttered and puts destructive form ops in one obvious place.
